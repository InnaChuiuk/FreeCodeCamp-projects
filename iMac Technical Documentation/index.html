Apple Intelligence

Apple Intelligence is the personal intelligence system that helps to write, express yourself, and get things done effortlessly. With groundbreaking privacy protections, it gives a peace of mind that no one else can access data — not even Apple.

  1. Writing Tools that can proofread text, rewrite different versions until the tone and wording are just right
  2. Priority notifications appear at the top of the stack, what to pay attention to at a glance
  3. Reduce Interruptions is an all-new Focus that understands the content of notifications and shows the ones that might need immediate attention
  4. Smart Reply in Mail to quickly draft an email response with all the right details
  5. Tap to reveal a summary of a long email in the Mail app and cut to the chase
Perfomance

iMac takes a leap in performance and AI capability with the M4 chip. A faster CPU makes everything you do even snappier. An advanced GPU with hardware-accelerated ray tracing delivers unbelievably realistic graphics.

The enhanced Media Engine lets you edit multiple streams of 4K video smoothly. Up to 32GB of faster unified memory enables frictionless gameplay and seamless multitasking across apps. And our powerful Neural Engine makes on-device Apple Intelligence soar.
